Climate Impact

El Niño's effects are vast, causing shifts in global weather patterns, rising temperatures, and extreme conditions. Here’s how it impacts our world.

Rising Temperatures

El Niño leads to significant temperature increases, affecting ecosystems and agriculture.

Droughts

Severe droughts hit various regions, reducing water supplies and impacting crops.

Floods
